Features

ScoutAI helps you research faster and organise your learning. Choose a level, ask a question, and get structured output.

Level-based answers

School = simple explanations. University = academic structure. Expert = deeper detail and nuance.

Summary first

ScoutAI starts with a short summary to quickly understand the topic before going deeper.

Detailed explanation

Structured answer with headings, clear steps, and examples where helpful.

Key points

Bullet-point takeaways so you can revise and build notes faster.

Essay / report outline

Optional structure to help you plan an assignment (intro → body → conclusion).

History (v1)

Chats are stored locally in your browser so you can return to previous research.
