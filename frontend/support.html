Support & FAQ

Quick answers for common questions. This section also supports Nielsen’s “Help and documentation” heuristic.

Which level should I choose?

School for simple explanations. University for academic structure. Expert for deeper technical detail.

Can ScoutAI do my assignment for me?

ScoutAI helps you learn and structure ideas. You should verify facts and write final work in your own words.

Why should I verify sources?

AI can make mistakes. For important topics, confirm claims with trusted sources (books, academic papers, official websites).

How do file uploads work (v1)?

In v1, uploading a PDF is a placeholder. Paste key text into the chat to summarise or explain it.
